Welcome to Tyche Poultry Farm!


At Tyche Poultry Farm, we are dedicated to providing high-quality poultry products to small and medium-scale farmers. Our farm specializes in rearing and selling healthy chicks, hens, and cocks for both rearing and consumption, alongside fresh eggs and broiler chickens for meat. With a focus on excellence and customer satisfaction, we strive to support farmers with top-notch products and expert advice, ensuring success in every venture.

  • 1-day-old chicks
  • 1 week to 1 month old chicks
  • Mature hens and roosters for rearing
  • Broiler chickens for meat
  • Fresh Eggs
